Recognized by Administration

Setting a Benchmark for Education in Sultanpur

Selection Library has caught the attention of local administration for its peaceful, disciplined, and study-centric environment where students continuously achieve success.

The SDM of Sultanpur recently visited and praised the institution, stating that Selection Library is not just paving the way for the bright future of students, but is setting a prime example of an excellent educational atmosphere in the district.

The administration also commended founder Shri Gaurav Srivastava as a successful emerging entrepreneur, noting that the institution plays an inspiring role in both education and social development.
